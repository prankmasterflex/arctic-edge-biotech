Our Technology

The Biochemistry of Odor Control

Our proprietary formulation targets the root cause of odor without disrupting the skin's natural barrier or microbiome diversity.

Mandelic Acid

An Alpha Hydroxy Acid (AHA) derived from bitter almonds that gently lowers dermal pH to inhibit odor-causing bacteria while promoting cellular turnover.

Zinc Ricinoleate

A zinc salt of ricinoleic acid extracted from castor oil. It traps and neutralizes odor molecules formed during bacterial decomposition of perspiration.

Prebiotic Support

Selective nutrients that feed beneficial skin flora (Staphylococcus epidermidis) while starving odor-producing strains (Corynebacterium), maintaining a healthy biome.
